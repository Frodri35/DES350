10 Principles For Good Design
1

1 Unique

Different from others work.

2 Aesthetic

Appealing to most people.

3 Fun

Engaging.

4 Consistent

Looks like you made it.

5 Minimal

Not too busy, simple and to the point.

6 Useful

It's more than just looks, you can use it.

7 It makes sense

The purpose of the design is clear.

8 Trendy

The design is up to date.

9 Artistic

The design consists of artistic traits or concepts.

10 Honest

The design dose not manipulate the consumer.
